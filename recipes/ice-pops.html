Lemonade Ice Pops

Description

These creamy lemonade ice pops are quick to make. I like them on the tart side, so I don't use too much sugar, but feel free to adjust the sweetness level to your liking. Anyone can prepare this recipe withour any experience of cooking.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Combine heavy whipping cream, lemon juice, milk, and sugar in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Pour into 4 ice pop molds
  3. Freeze until solid, at lease 4 hours or overnight
