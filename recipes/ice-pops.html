Ice Pops

Description

Indulge in a delightful and healthy treat with this effortless ice pop recipe! This recipe utilizes the natural goodness of fresh fruits, yogurt, and a touch of sweetness (like honey) to create popsicles that are not only delicious but also a healthier alternative to store-bought options.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Lemon juice, milk, and sugar in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Pour into 4 ice pop molds
  3. Freeze until solid, at lease 4 hours or overnight
