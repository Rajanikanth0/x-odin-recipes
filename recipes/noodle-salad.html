Noodle Salad

Description

This quick noodle salad is a refreshing and flavorful dish perfect for a speedy meal or side, especially during warmer months.

Ingredients

  • 1 angel hair pasta
  • 1/2 cup sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup noodles
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Once the water is boiling, stir in the angel hair pasta, and return to a boil. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has cooked through, but is still firm to the bite.
  2. Chili oil and sugar in a large bowl. Toss the pasta in the dressing, then sprinkle with sesame seeds, green onion, and bell pepper. Serve warm, or cover.
