City cavi

Description

This quick city cavi recipe offers a delightful and refreshing way to enjoy seafood, especially during warm weather. It's a faster alternative to traditional ceviche, which relies solely on citrus juice to "cook" the fish overnight. Instead, this recipe employs a brief poaching process for the seafood before it's marinated, ensuring a quicker preparation time while still delivering delicious results.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced seeded tomato
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin

Steps

  1. A large bowl with ice and cold water; set aside. Remove and discard tough side muscles from scallops.
  2. Return water to a boil. opaque and pink, 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer shrimp using a slotted spoon to the same bowl of ice water to stop the cooking
  3. Pat dry with paper towels. Transfer to a glass, ceramic, or other non-reactive bowl. Stir in lime juice and orange juice. refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  4. Leaving it just moist. Add bell peppers, tomato, red onion, cilantro, serano, orange zest, cumin, cayenne pepper, and salt.
  5. Fold in avocado and drizzle with oil just before serving. Serve in martini glasses or stemmed glasses.
